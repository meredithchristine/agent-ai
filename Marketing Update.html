  • Given that Google Ads is our dominant paid channel, a meaningful share of these "undefined" sign-ups are likely ad-driven users whose UTM parameters were stripped
  • Possible causes: redirect chains, cross-domain tracking gaps, landing page issues, or UTM params not appended to all ad URLs
